About the Artist

Drawing from his professional life as an architect, Andrew Knight brings a unique structural understanding to his pen and ink illustrations. This deep appreciation for form, light, and precision informs his work, which focuses on architectural landmarks and moments of quiet contemplation.

Each piece is a testament to this dual passion, a labor of love composed of thousands of individual dots that build a rich tapestry of shadow and line. Welcome to his studio.
